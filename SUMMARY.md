**Labeled Surgical Tools and Images** is a dataset for an object detection task. It is used in the medical industry. 

The dataset consists of 2620 images with 3639 labeled objects belonging to 4 different classes including *curved mayo scissor*, *scalpel nยบ4*, *straight dissection clamp*, and other: *straight mayo scissor*. There are 2 splits in the dataset: *train* (1834 images) and *test* (786 images). Additionally, objects with ***occlusion*** are marked. Explore them in supervisely.. The dataset was released in 2018 by the University of Coimbra, Portugal.
